# Google Play Store — Required Visual Assets Checklist

All images must be PNG or JPEG (no transparency for feature graphic / screenshots).

## App Icon
- [ ] **Hi-res icon**: 512 x 512 px, PNG, 32-bit
  - Must NOT have rounded corners (Google adds them automatically)
  - Background: #080d1a (dark navy)
  - Foreground: the "A" logo mark with blue/cyan gradient

## Feature Graphic (mandatory)
- [ ] **Feature Graphic**: 1024 x 500 px, PNG or JPEG
  - This appears at the top of your Play Store listing
  - Suggested: dark (#080d1a) background with WealthIQ logo + tagline
  - "India's AI Financial Advisor"

## Screenshots (minimum 2, recommend 4-8)
Phone screenshots: **1080 x 1920 px** (portrait, 16:9)
- [ ] Screenshot 1 — Home / Dashboard (Financial Health Score)
- [ ] Screenshot 2 — AI Advice screen
- [ ] Screenshot 3 — Tax Estimation tool
- [ ] Screenshot 4 — Profile / Subscription plans

Optional — 7-inch tablet: 1200 x 1920 px
Optional — 10-inch tablet: 1920 x 1200 px

## Short Promo Video (optional but recommended)
- YouTube URL, 30 seconds to 2 minutes
- Shows the app in action on a real device
