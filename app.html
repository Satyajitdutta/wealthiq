FIRE = Financial Independence, Retire Early
The 25× rule says: if your investments are 25 times your annual expenses, they can sustain you indefinitely — because a well-diversified portfolio historically returns ~4%/yr (the "Safe Withdrawal Rate"). Withdraw 4% of ₹1 Cr = ₹4 L/yr = your expenses covered forever, without touching the principal.

Your FIRE Number = your current annual expenses × 25

What comes under Other deductions?

Financial Independence
FIRE = Financial Independence, Retire Early
Your expenses today will be higher at retirement due to inflation. Artha-IQ inflates your current expenses to what they will actually cost at age 60, then calculates the corpus needed to sustain that lifestyle using your real return rate (return minus inflation). This gives you a realistic, not underestimated, target.
FIRE Number = Inflation-adjusted annual expenses at age 60 / real return rate
