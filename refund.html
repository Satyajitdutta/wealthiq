Cancellation & Refund Policy

Effective date: 1 April 2026  ·  PITHONIX AI INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED

WealthIQ is a digital subscription service operated by PITHONIX AI INDIA PRIVATE LIMITED. As a digital product, our refund policy is as follows.

1. Cancellation

  • You may cancel your subscription at any time from within the app or by emailing info@pithonix.ai.
  • Upon cancellation, your plan remains active until the end of the current billing month.
  • You will not be charged again after cancellation.
  • Your data and profile will be retained for 90 days after cancellation, after which they may be deleted.

2. Refunds

Standard policy: We do not offer refunds for the current billing period once a payment has been processed, as the service is immediately accessible upon payment.

3. Exceptions — When We Do Refund

  • Technical failure: If the service was completely inaccessible for more than 48 continuous hours due to our fault during your paid period, you are eligible for a pro-rated refund.
  • Duplicate payment: If you were charged twice for the same plan in error, the duplicate charge will be fully refunded.
  • Within 7 days of first payment: New subscribers who have not meaningfully used the service may request a full refund within 7 days of their first payment — no questions asked.

4. How to Request a Refund

Email info@pithonix.ai with:

  • Your registered email address
  • Razorpay payment ID (found in your payment confirmation email)
  • Reason for the refund request

Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days to the original payment method.

5. Free Trial

WealthIQ offers 5 free sessions before any payment is required. No credit card is needed for the trial. We encourage you to explore the app fully during the trial before subscribing.
